  • Poetry Sep 01, 2024

    Afloat

    It feels like I have forgotten something, and I keep drifting between peace and emptiness.

    It feels like I've forgotten something,
    Something very important,
    At times I find myself in the darkness in solitude,
    As it engulfs my being like a comforting velvet shroud,
    Or walking beneath the canvas of the starry night,
    Admiring its beauty as the stars twinkle,
    Without a thought in mind,
    Afloat amongst the clouds in the sky.
  • Poetry Aug 03, 2024

    Walk in the Rain

    Grey clouds overhead, wet boots below, and the soft rhythm of rain in between.

    From glittering droplets of pouring rain,
    To puddles on the pavement, shimmering silver.
    Grey clouds hang overhead in the dim sky,
    The humid wind blows
    Dripping lightly through my soaked hair,
    Drip drip
    Down to the flat soles of my boots.
  • Poetry Jul 18, 2024

    My Silent Fortress

    A self-built bastion that protects me, but also keeps me from the stars.

    It's empty. Just a vast expanse,
    Stretching on endlessly.
    Darkness everywhere,
    Save for the galaxy painted on the ceiling.
    A self-inflicted prison,
    Protecting me from the dangers of the outside world.
    I want to explore the outside world.
    But I remain still.
